Home Waves & Shells Research
This website is a digital interpretation of a beach of a place called Karioitahi beach where I spent my lockdown. I looked at my daily routine and going to the beach is what came up the most. So I decided to do a project on it. I started to explored the shells round Karioitahi I then proceeded to:
  • Ink the shells and stamp them to see what negative and positive shapes they would make on paper
  • Teasted out scan scan them multiple times
